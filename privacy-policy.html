Soumya Tech File Manager

File Manager & Cloud Integration

1. Introduction

Welcome to Soumya Tech File Manager. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. Total Storage is a file manager application designed to help you organize your local files and manage your cloud storage efficiently.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy complies with Google Play Developer Program Policies.

This policy applies specifically to the Android application published under the name Total Storage on the Google Play Store.

2. Google Drive Access

Total Storage integrates with Google Drive to provide seamless file management capabilities. Our application requests access to your Google Drive files only upon your explicit consent and interaction. We access your Google Drive data solely to enable you to:

  • Browse: View folders and files.
  • Upload: Upload local files to Google Drive.
  • Download: Save files from Google Drive to your device.
  • Delete: Remove files or folders only when you explicitly request it.

3. Data Usage

  • No External Servers: Files are accessed directly between your device and Google servers.
  • No Background Access: The app does not access files without your action.
  • No Data Mining: File contents are never scanned or analyzed.
  • User-Initiated Only: All actions require user interaction.

3A. Device Permissions

Total Storage requires certain device permissions strictly to provide file management features:

  • Storage Access: To browse, read, write, upload, download, and delete files on your device.
  • Internet Access: Required only to connect to cloud services such as Google Drive.

These permissions are never used for tracking, advertising, or background data collection.

Permissions are requested at runtime and can be revoked anytime from system settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Secure Token Storage: OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device.
  • Encrypted Communication: All data transfers use HTTPS.
  • Local-First Design: Metadata is cached locally for performance.

5. Data Sharing

  • No sale, trade, or rent of user data.
  • No sharing with advertisers or analytics providers.
  • No tracking or profiling.

6. User Control

  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time.
  • You can revoke access via Google Account Permissions.

7. Third-Party Services & Compliance

“The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.”

Google Drive access is requested only to enable core file management features and is never used for advertising or analytics.

8. Children’s Privacy

Total Storage is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
